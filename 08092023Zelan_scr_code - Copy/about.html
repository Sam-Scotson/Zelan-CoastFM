About

Who we are

Zelan was started on the West Coast in 2010 and has connected many of its rural users to broadband with its fixed wireless network, beaming data to places other internet service providers can't or won't go.

With a customer-centric approach and exceptional service, Zelan has become the go-to small ISP, transforming lives and bridging the digital divide in the region.

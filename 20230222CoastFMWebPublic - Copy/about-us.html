ABOUT US

Founded 25 years ago Coast FM is the only locally owned live on-air station broadcasting on the West Coast.

Playing today’s best music mix.

Our listeners love us because we engage with them proactively, consistently deliver local news and event updates,
local sport and community messages and great competition prizes.
We engage with our audience on social media (Facebook and Instagram) Coast FM streams globally too.  

We’re also the only radio station with local studios; a huge plus!
The team at Coast FM are committed to providing the best community news, home town sports and community events coverage for our region.
We also play today’s best music mix!

We pride ourselves on being local, entertaining and informative, covering the West Coast from Karamea to Fox Glacier,
playing music from the 80s, 90s and now.
